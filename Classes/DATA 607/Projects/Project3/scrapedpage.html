                          Job description

                          #LoveWhereYouWork


                          We're looking for people who love turning data into gold.


                          Are you someone who solves hard problems by creatively obtaining, scrubbing, mathematically exploring, modeling and interpreting data?


                          Do you know enough about information retrieval, machine learning and statistics to be dangerous?


                          Are you a hands-on implementer, ready to learn new models and technologies to turn your ideas into solutions used by companies around the world?


                          If your answer is “Yes, show me the data!” then this is the job for you.


                          As data scientists, we work on some of the most exciting areas of computer and information science, including intelligent information extraction, recommendation systems, visualizations and cognitive agents. Moreover, we support core product needs and drive service delivery. You will work in a cross-functional team with Software Engineers, Product Owners and Customer Success.


                          Interested? 


                          Here is what we are looking for:


                          • Master’s degree (Ph.D. preferred) in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Physics or Engineering.
                          • Hands-on experience on tweaking machine-learning to specific in-house predictive problems.
                          • Technical skills to be able to implement what you think (e.g. R, Python scripts, complex SQL queries, Julia) and interest in new AI technologies.
                          • The drive and discipline to work in a product environment (version control, QA, automation, service delivery)
                          • Creativity and good taste in problems with business impact.
                          • A collegial disposition and strong communication skills.
                          • At least 1 year of relevant experience.
                          • Fluency in English.

                          About us
                          Pick the Right Price with Just One Click. Maximize revenue during every promotional cycle using artificial intelligence Ranked by CIO Review in Top 100 Most Promising BigData Solution Providers. Selected by SAP for its exclusive SAP.io foundry in Berlin. The leader in applying artificial intelligence accurately predicts retailer sales for every SKU at any price, so you can increase your profits and outsmart competitors. Wise Athena provides a leading software platform for the consumer goods industry. With a suite of pricing and trade promotion optimization software: constraint-based predictive optimization, optimization through iterative scenarios, prediction of “best” price and promotion option, cannibalization and price ratio effects. Your Biggest Challenge… Tired of trying to process large amounts of data sets with limited technology support? During a rushed promotional cycle there is never enough time. Not to mention that retailers today are demanding than ever, requiring more promotions, at a rapid pace. How can you keep up while aligning your pricing and trade promotions in a way that maximizes margin and profits? Our Goal: Leverage artificial intelligence to help CPG companies predict the right price in just one click. We have reimagined every element to create a precise, affordable and easy-to-use tool that redefines the future of pricing for CPGs. Wise Athena Analyzes 1000’s of Data Points So You Sell More Nothing can beat thousands of brains working on each of your data points simultaneously, and that is precisely what AI does for you. By analyzing your competitors’ actions in conjunction with your product’s performance, Wise Athena helps you choose the best pricing; so you maximize your sales and profit during every promotional cycle.
